RUN

DISCLAIMER: THIS IS NOT A MOVIE ABOUT RUNNING!!!

Run a highschool senior full of passion for writing, is faced with harsh obstacles. He wants to be a writer, but his parents are completely against his dreams. They want their marvelous son to apply to johnson and wales and major in culinary arts. Run also has a pet Goat. This Goat also causes him to face another problem because his girlfriend does not like Goats and will leave him if he insists to keep it.

  • Will Run become a baker or continue his dream of becoming a writer?
  • Will he go against the family tradition and not become a baker?
  • Will he chose to keep his pet Goat over his girlfriend?
  • Or will he agree to give away his Goat who was his bestfriend?
